The Academics World 843rd International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Soft Computing (ICAISC) aims to bring together leading academic scientists, researchers and research scholars to exchange and share their experiences and research results about all aspects of Artificial Intelligence and Soft Computing. It also provides the premier interdisciplinary forum for researchers, practitioners and educators to present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, and concerns, practical challenges encountered and the solutions adopted in the fields of Artificial Intelligence and Soft Computing.
